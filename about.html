Behind the work

Marketing strategist jamming with code — calm design, clear words, tidy CSS.

I've spent the last 9 years gathering a mix of professional experiences, and for the past 5, I've been deep in the world of design — working with startups and established brands to bring ideas to life. Design, for me, isn't just a buzzword or a business trend it's something that inspires me daily.

It's in the small, quiet moments: the layout of a train ticket machine that just works, the way an app knows what you need before you do, or the satisfying click of a well-designed lid.The funny thing is, the best design often goes unnoticed it's so intuitive, it disappears into the background.

And maybe that's the point. In a world full of noise, I'm drawn to the simplicity and elegance of things that just work. It's that rare space where business meets creativity, and when they don't naturally meet, I enjoy being the bridge.
